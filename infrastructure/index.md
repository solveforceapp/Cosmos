# Infrastructure

Infrastructure is the foundation of the digital and physical cosmos. This section explores the systems, networks, and technologies that enable connectivity, computation, and energy flow at every scale.

## Overview

Infrastructure underpins:
- Cloud computing
- Data centers
- Edge and quantum networks
- Energy grids

1. Physical (cables, servers, power)
2. Network (routing, switching, protocols)
3. Virtualization (cloud, containers)
4. Application (services, APIs)
