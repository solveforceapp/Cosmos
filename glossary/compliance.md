# Compliance

## Definition

Compliance is the practice of adhering to laws, regulations, standards, and policies relevant to business operations. In IT, cybersecurity, and telecommunications, compliance ensures organizations operate ethically, legally, and securely.

## How It Works

Compliance involves identifying applicable rules, implementing controls, monitoring activities, and documenting evidence. Audits, risk assessments, and training are key components. Non-compliance can result in fines, reputational damage, or legal action.

## Real-World Application

Compliance is critical in healthcare (HIPAA), finance (SOX), and data privacy (GDPR). Organizations must adapt to evolving regulations and demonstrate accountability to customers and regulators.
