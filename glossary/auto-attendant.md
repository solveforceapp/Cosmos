# Auto-Attendant

## Definition

An auto-attendant is a telephony system feature that automatically answers incoming calls and routes them to the appropriate extension, department, or voicemail box based on user input. Auto-attendants streamline call handling, reduce wait times, and enhance customer experience by providing self-service options.

## How It Works

When a call comes in, the auto-attendant greets the caller with a recorded message and presents a menu of options (e.g., "Press 1 for Sales, 2 for Support"). ## Real-World Application

Auto-attendants are used by businesses, government agencies, and service providers to manage high call volumes, ensure efficient routing, and provide 24/7 availability. They reduce the need for human receptionists and support remote or distributed teams.
