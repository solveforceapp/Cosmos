# Data Mining

## Definition

Data mining is the process of analyzing large datasets to discover patterns, trends, and useful information for decision-making and business intelligence. It uses statistical, machine learning, and visualization techniques.

## How It Works

Data mining tools sift through data to identify correlations, anomalies, and predictive insights. Results inform marketing, fraud detection, and scientific research.

## Real-World Application

Data mining is used by retailers, banks, and healthcare providers to understand customers, detect fraud, and improve outcomes.

Imagine a prospector panning for gold in a river of information, searching for valuable nuggets.

## Why It Matters

Data mining turns raw data into actionable knowledge, driving smarter decisions.
