# Data Protection

## Definition

Data protection is the set of strategies, policies, and technologies used to safeguard data from loss, corruption, theft, or unauthorized access. It ensures data availability, integrity, and confidentiality.

## How It Works

Data protection includes backups, encryption, access controls, and disaster recovery plans. Organizations assess risks and implement layered defenses.

## Real-World Application

Data protection is vital for compliance, business continuity, and customer trust. It is used in every industry, from healthcare to retail.

Imagine a vault with multiple locks, alarms, and guards, keeping your valuables safe from harm.

## Why It Matters

Data protection is the backbone of digital trust and resilience.
