# Hosted PBX

## Definition

A hosted PBX is a cloud-based phone system where the service provider manages the PBX equipment and services offsite, delivering business telephony over the internet. Hosted PBX solutions offer flexibility, scalability, and cost savings for organizations.

## How It Works

## Real-World Application

Businesses of all sizes use hosted PBX to support remote work, multi-site operations, and unified communications. Providers offer advanced features and integrations with CRM, email, and collaboration tools.

## Entertaining Example

Imagine a virtual receptionist who answers calls from anywhere in the world, or a phone system that grows with your business at the click of a button. Or picture a switchboard in the cloud, connecting people across continents.

## Why It Matters

Hosted PBX modernizes business communications, enabling agility, productivity, and customer satisfaction.
