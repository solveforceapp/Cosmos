# Cybersecurity

## Definition

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from digital attacks, unauthorized access, and damage. It uses technologies, processes, and policies to ensure confidentiality, integrity, and availability.

## How It Works

Cybersecurity measures include firewalls, encryption, access controls, and monitoring. Organizations assess risks, train staff, and respond to incidents to minimize impact.

## Real-World Application

Cybersecurity is vital for businesses, governments, and individuals. It protects sensitive data, supports compliance, and prevents cyberattacks.

Imagine a digital fortress with layers of defenses, keeping out intruders and safeguarding treasures.

## Entertaining Example

Picture a superhero team defending a city from digital villains, always ready to stop the next attack.

## Why It Matters

Cybersecurity is the foundation of trust and innovation in the digital world.
