# Bluetooth

## Definition

Bluetooth is a wireless technology standard that enables the exchange of voice, data, and multimedia between devices over short distances using radio waves. Bluetooth simplifies connectivity, eliminates cables, and supports a wide range of applications in personal and professional environments.

## How It Works

Bluetooth devices use low-power radio signals in the 2.4 GHz band to communicate. Devices pair through a discovery and authentication process, forming secure connections for data transfer. Bluetooth supports profiles for audio, file sharing, health monitoring, and more.

## Real-World Application

Bluetooth is found in smartphones, headphones, speakers, wearables, cars, and smart home devices. It enables hands-free calling, wireless music streaming, fitness tracking, and device automation. Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) extends battery life for IoT applications.
