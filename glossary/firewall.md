# Firewall

## Definition

A firewall is a security system that monitors and controls incoming and outgoing network traffic based on predetermined security rules. Firewalls protect networks from unauthorized access, malware, and cyberattacks.

## How It Works

Firewalls can be hardware, software, or cloud-based. They inspect data packets, block suspicious activity, and enforce policies. Advanced firewalls offer intrusion prevention, VPN support, and application-layer filtering.

## Real-World Application

Businesses, schools, and individuals use firewalls to safeguard computers, servers, and IoT devices. Firewalls are essential for regulatory compliance and risk management.
