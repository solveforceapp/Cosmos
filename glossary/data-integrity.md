# Data Integrity

## Definition

Data integrity is the accuracy, consistency, and reliability of data throughout its lifecycle. It is protected from unauthorized alteration or corruption, ensuring trustworthy information for decision-making.

## How It Works

Integrity is maintained through validation, checksums, access controls, and audit trails. Organizations monitor data for errors and unauthorized changes.

## Real-World Application

Data integrity is vital in healthcare, finance, and research. It supports compliance, analytics, and operational efficiency.

Imagine a chain of custody for digital evidence, ensuring nothing is lost or tampered with.

## Entertaining Example

Picture a notary who certifies every document, guaranteeing its authenticity and accuracy.

## Why It Matters

Data integrity is the foundation of reliable systems and informed decisions.
